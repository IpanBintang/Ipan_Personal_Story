My Hobbies & Interests

Outside of my studies, I make it a point to engage in activities that recharge my energy and bring me joy. Hobbies are not just pastimes — they are a window into a person's character, creativity, and values. Here are the things I love to do most in my free time.

📷 Photography

Photography is my favourite hobby. I started taking photos when I was in secondary school using just my phone, and I have grown to appreciate the art of capturing moments, light, and emotion. I particularly enjoy street photography and nature photography.

I am currently saving up for a proper DSLR camera. For now, I use my smartphone and experiment with different editing apps. You can see some of my work on the Gallery page.

📚 Reading

I have loved reading since I was a child. Books open up worlds that you cannot experience any other way. My favourite genres include self-improvement, science fiction, and biographies.

Some books that have truly impacted my life:

  • Atomic Habits by James Clear
  • The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho
  • Sapiens by Yuval Noah Harari

Learn more about my favourite authors at Goodreads.

🏓 Badminton

Badminton is my go-to sport. I play at least twice a week with my friends and housemates at the campus sports hall. It is a great way to stay active while having fun and building friendships.

I have been playing since I was 10 years old. My father taught me the basics, and ever since then, it has been a family tradition. I especially admire Lee Chong Wei — a true legend of Malaysian badminton. Read about his achievements at Wikipedia.

🍳 Cooking

Cooking is something I discovered during the pandemic, and I have not stopped since. There is something deeply satisfying about creating a meal from scratch and sharing it with people you care about. I enjoy cooking Malaysian traditional dishes like nasi goreng, rendang, and mee goreng.

"Cooking is love made visible." — I truly believe that. I often cook for my family during weekends when I go back home. It is our special bonding time.
